Description

It's nearly Valentine's Day at the Norkleeson Plant Nursery and that means love is in the air, not just for humans but for the flowers as well. Brought in as the newest flower, learn the stories of the nursery's flowers and perhaps find a special someone to call your own.

History

This game was created as part of Passionfruit Jam 2021, a local college game jam. For a few weeks after, it was further developed, then released as an updated version.

Features

  • 4 Unique and Expressive Flowers to Meet
  • 4 Unique Routes To Complete
  • 6 Original Compositions To Listen To

My Work

About the Process

One of the first games I worked on as a confident game developer. I attended the 2021 Passionfruit Jam remotely, giving me the chance to take on a game project even while working on co-op in another state. As the sole programmer and one of three producers on the project, time after work was spent coordinating with team members over Discord to ensure that scripts, art, and music had proper direction and got done on time. It was an amazing chance to interact with all the elements that lead to a well-made game and I'm proud of how we were able to make it all come together.
