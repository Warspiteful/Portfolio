Description

Dogfight! is a space-based combat game where players battle in a free-for-all battle to be the last man standing. Play combat cards from your hand to fire weapons or ready shields, and position yourself strategically in an evolving battlefield. Defeat your enemies through clever predictions, navigate space wreckage, and get revenge on your friends as floating space debris!

History

This game was created as the final project to GAME 2500 - Foundations of Game Design
