Description

“Wander” is a fun little game about exploration, meeting a wacky cast of characters, and finding your way back home, with cute art and calming music. It aims to capture the commotion but also the serenity of living being in a big city.

History

About the Process

One of the first games I worked on as a confident game developer. I attended the 2021 Passionfruit Jam remotely, giving me the chance to take on a game project even while working on co-op in another state. As the sole programmer and one of three producers on the project, time after work was spent coordinating with team members over Discord to ensure that scripts, art, and music had proper direction and got done on time. It was an amazing chance to interact with all the elements that lead to a well-made game and I'm proud of how we were able to make it all come together.
